Who We Are

We are a young couple and we decided to create our hotel where we could go in and out very quickly. So the walls in the kitchen have windows and a view over the valley. And because I needed to be in front of the cookers to manage the kitchen, it seemed logical to get someone who could work back and forth by car. So we started working on the office and the staff and before long we had an entirely new chef coming on board. So we now have four chefs working in the hotel and two chefs in the café on the ground floor.
