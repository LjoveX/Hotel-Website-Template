History of KTB Hotel

Our hotel was born in 1964, it's one of the oldest in town. Originally it was a house of a factory worked, he used to live here with his family. During the 50s and the roaring years, he decide to move in a bigger home near the center of the city. For that, in his memory we decide to leave untouched the structure of the rooms, we just renewed the furnitures and the bathrooms, to let you live a full experience of the 40s but without forgiving all the comforts of the modern era.
